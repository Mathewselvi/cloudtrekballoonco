About Us

Cloud Trek Balloon Co

Cloud Trek Balloon Co is Munnar's premier hot air balloon experience, offering breathtaking views over the region's iconic tea plantations, misty hills, and rolling peaks. Nestled in the heart of Kerala's Western Ghats, our balloon rides provide a safe and peaceful way to explore one of South India's most scenic destinations.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply chasing adventure, Cloud Trek Balloon Co combines safety, comfort, and natural beauty to create unforgettable memories above the clouds.
